IEC 62443-4-2 Certified RTU Platform

As Technical Lead and Product Owner, I led the design and implementation of security module for general-purpose Remote Terminal Unit (RTU) platform that successfully achieved IEC 62443-4-2 certification - the international standard for secure components in industrial automation and control systems.

The first certification phase focused on IEC 104 protocol security, ensuring compliance with cybersecurity requirements for communication, authentication, and data integrity.

My Role

  • Directed the certification project end-to-end, from requirements mapping to successful audit.

  • Coordinated a cross-functional team of software engineers and security experts to implement security features.

  • Personally oversaw all technical aspects of IEC 62443-4-2 compliance (authentication, authorization, secure communication, logging, user management, session security, system hardening).

  • Acted as the bridge between engineering and business goals, aligning the product roadmap with certification milestones.

    • Achievements

  • Successfully passed the independent certification audit, obtaining the IEC 62443-4-2 certificate (from AFTA) for the RTU platform.

  • Delivered a certified product deployed in the field, providing industrial customers with a trusted and standards-compliant solution.

  • Strengthened the company's position in the industrial cybersecurity market by embedding international security standards into the product line.

    • Designed and implemented a custom embedded firewall supporting industrial protocols, with web-based configuration and real-time monitoring.
