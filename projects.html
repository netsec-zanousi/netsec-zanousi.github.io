    • Resolving Network Congestion in Industrial DCS Systems

    Role: Technical Lead

    In the industrial Distributed Control System (DCS) environments, we faced a persistent network congestion issue. The congestion was caused by improperly configured switches, which led to significant packet loss in Foundation Fieldbus network. The source of the problem was unclear at first, and it threatened the stability of critical industrial operations.

    Investigation & R&D

    I conducted intensive research and experimentation across multiple industrial sites. Through detailed analysis and trial, I identified that the issue stemmed from improper IGMP (Internet Group Management Protocol) handling in the network.

    Achievements

    The result was a stable and reliable industrial network. Packet loss was completely eliminated, and the system returned to normal operation. This improvement significantly increased the resilience and availability of critical DCS communications.
